01 · Mission

We build the cognitive layer that surrounds the model.

Most frontier labs are racing to build a bigger brain. We're building everything around it — the memory, the perception, the scope control, the evidence management, and the feedback loops that turn a powerful model into a genuinely cognitive system.

We call this the external cognitive infrastructure of agentic intelligence. It's not about the weights. It's about what happens before and after the forward pass.

Our work is documented in our research thesis and published as findings appear in our field notes.

02 · Philosophy

Long bets, published honestly.

Research moves fast when you publish only wins. We don't do that. Our field notes include negative results, surprises, and things we got wrong — because that's what real research looks like.

Each program is a long-horizon bet on a specific mechanism we think is necessary for machine consciousness. We run them in parallel, and they cross-pollinate. A result in one frequently changes the framing in another.

03 · Values

How we operate.

01

Independence first

No external investors dictating research direction. The programs we run are the ones we believe are genuinely important — not the ones that are easiest to pitch.

02

Ship everything

Every system we build runs on eloai.dev subdomains. Research that doesn't run in production is theory. We build, we deploy, we observe.

03

Honest publishing

Findings go up as they happen, positive or negative. The field notes are unfiltered — including the experiments that taught us what doesn't work.

04 · Infrastructure

One domain. Every project.

All production systems run on the eloai.dev domain. Each subdomain is a self-contained service — independently versioned, observable, and reachable through a single TLS endpoint.

This makes it easy to route new side projects and AI tools into the same trusted namespace as our core research stack.
